E-Commerce Website

Project Overview

DasWearables is a fictitious e-commerce website that sells a variety of wearable products. This was a group project that I and 3 other students created while attending the Full-Stack Development program at Concordia University.

Explanation

Utilizing static data provided by instructors (products, companies etc.), my group had to create an e-commerce website from scratch. All of the work was taken on with each team member having specific tasks to complete, from developing the frontend to the backend.

Group Objective

As this was our first time working in a group setting, our main goal was to stay extremely organized and communicate efficiently with the team. During the entirety of the project, we used a Trello to spread the responsibility of tasks, and set up daily calls to track progress of our work.

Our clear communication during the duration of the project aloud us to create a polished and usable website for users to purchase products. I found this to be a fantastic learning experience, as this was all our first time developing a project in a group setting!
