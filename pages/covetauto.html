Covet Automotive

Overview

Covet Automotive is a marketplace for consumers looking to get authentic, quality, curated used automotive parts and products. This was a college assignment that turned into a personal project to practice my UX and UI design skills.

Design Objective

Introducing an application that can curate and organize users to buy and sell a variety of automotive products is fairly unique within its self. So, coming up with a unique and original interface that is extremely user friendly proved to be a challenge.

Results

Created the high-fidelity designs of highest priority screens that effectively highlight the user journey.

With that being said, I first and foremost wanted the app to be functional without any friction or confusion.

Persona

I created a persona and task flow based on a user scenario. This gave me the ability to understand the users point of view, especially in the preliminary designs of the highest priority screens.

Wireframe

I really wanted to focus on the users and how they can effectively buy and sell automotive products in the most simple way possible. The less steps to complete a task, the less errors. Keeping a minimalist design was key to having an effective and usable interface.

Mockup

I wanted to create an attractive, clean, and easy to navigate interface that users can quickly and effectively use right off the bat. The rich blue colour compliments many areas of the design while highlighting some of the key functional features for improved usability and navigation. The prominent colour also gives the app identity users can become familiar with.

Future Considerations

Although I would have liked to user test with a larger demographic of users to gain a better understanding of who I am designing for, it was a great exercise that enabled me to understand how to effectively use UX principles to create an effective and engaging interface.
