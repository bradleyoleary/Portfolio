To-Do App

Overview & Project Goals

Myself and a colleague, François, took it upon our selves to improve on our development skills and created a fun little "To-Do" app using backend and frontend technologies. Our goal was to create a simplistic, two page To-Do app where users can assign tasks to specific dates.

Explanation

Users have the ability to create, define, and set tasks to a specific date. Since there is a database connected to the app, all tasks will be saved to the date they were assigned to. The beauty of this app is the simplicity!

Design Objective

Although just a two page app, we wanted a super minimal design and easy to use interface. We went with a bright and inviting colour pallet for areas of importance, and kept the rest of the app free of colour.
