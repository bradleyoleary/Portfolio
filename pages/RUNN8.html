RUNN8

Overview

RUNN8 Athletic Shuttles is a local ride-share service for student athletes. They offer parents and athletic organizations a solution for shuttling student athletes between home and athletic facilities. Although the website development is in progress, check them out at runn8.com.

Design Objective

My objective was to create a homepage that is easy to navigate, and encourages users to sign up.

Results

Designed a landing page for the website based off market research and wireframes.

Methodologies Tools
Persona Development Adobe XD
Task Flows
Wireframes & Mockups

Research & Analysis

I conducted a competitive analysis of similar platforms who have a huge influence in the ride-sharing industry such as Uber, Lyft, Curb, etc. I came to the conclusion that promptly displaying what the product is about is key for users so sign up.

Who am I designing for?

I created a persona based on a user scenario. I love creating personas, as it gives me the ability to see through the eyes of the user and prioritize what matters most.

Wireframe

I really wanted to focus on the users and how they can effectively buy and sell automotive products in the most simple way possible. The less steps to complete a task, the less errors. Keeping a minimalist design was key to having an effective and usable interface.

Mockup

I wanted to create an attractive, clean, and easy to navigate interface that users can quickly and effectively use right off the bat. The rich blue colour compliments many areas of the design while highlighting some of the key functional features for improved usability and navigation. The prominent colour also gives the app identity users can become familiar with.

Future Considerations

Although I would have liked to user test with a larger demographic of users to gain a better understanding of who I am designing for, it was a great exercise that enabled me to understand how to effectively use UX principles to create an effective and engaging interface.
