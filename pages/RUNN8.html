Research & Analysis

I conducted a competitive analysis of similar platforms that have a huge influence in the ride-sharing industry such as Uber, Lyft, Curb, etc. I concluded that a clear product goal and minimalist design and is the key for high conversion rates in regards to sign-ups.

Who am I designing for?

I created a persona based on a user scenario. I truly enjoy creating personas, as it gives me the ability to see through the eyes of the user and prioritize what matters most in terms of usability.

Wireframe

Looking back at my design goal, I knew a simplistic layout for the homepage will be very important for the user demographic of RUNN8. Short text with plenty of CTA's are placed on the landing page, saving the more in depth content for informational pages such as Our Company and Safety.
